QR Code stands for "Quick Response" Code.
While they may look simple, QR codes are capable of storing lots of data. QR codes are frequently used to track information about products in a supply chain.
Therefore, We have choosen this as our Minor project because in the packaging field the use of QR codes is increasing continuously.
